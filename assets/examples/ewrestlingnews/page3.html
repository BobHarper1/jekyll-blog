The long awaited fight between Rashad Evans and Quinton "Rampage" Jackson is signed. UFC made the announcement on Thursday that the Ultimate Fighter Season Ten coaches will go to war at UFC 114 on May 29th from Las Vegas.

The fight was originally booked for December's UFC 107 PPV from Memphis, but Rampage went AWOL to do his Mr. T thing for the A-Team movie. The two built the fight up throughout their time on TUF, with both launching comments at the other on multiple occasions.
